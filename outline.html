Outline

The goal of the research group on Stochastic Modelling is to have regular (weekly) meetings among PhDs, PostDocs and junior researchers on several topics related to the thorey of stochastic processes and their application in particular in the fields of economics and finance.

During each meeting, a published (or preprint version of) paper will be presented by a participant, who will also start the discussion which is open to all participants The aim is to strengthen the background of the participants in stochastic modelling, and to provide an informal environment for fruitful discussions. Presentations of working papers by the participants are allowed.

The scheduling (including the choice of the paper to be discussed and the presenter) is decided by the coordinators (prof. Roberto Casarin, prof. Pietro Dindo) upon proposal by the participants.

For further information, contact Matteo Iacopini.
